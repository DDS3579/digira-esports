About Digira Esports

Building Nepal's competitive gaming future, one tournament at a time

Our Story

Digira Esports was founded by passionate teens in Kathmandu who saw the incredible potential of Nepal's gaming community. What started as a small group of friends organizing local tournaments has grown into one of Nepal's most promising esports organizations.

We believe that esports in Nepal deserves the same recognition and infrastructure as traditional sports. Our mission is to create a professional, inclusive platform where gamers can compete, grow, and showcase their talents.

Through dedication and community support, we've hosted numerous successful tournaments, connected hundreds of gamers, and built a reputation for fair, exciting competitive events.
